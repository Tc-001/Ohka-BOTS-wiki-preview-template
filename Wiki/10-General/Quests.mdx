# Quests
Story Quests follow the linear storyline of Ohka BOTS, taking you through each Sector maps and being rewarded as you power up enough to complete higher level maps. Story Quests reward players with Coin items, Coupons, Experience, Gigas and more. All Story Quests have a minimum required level you must reach before you can unlock the Quest.

Daily Quests refresh at midnight EST with various rewards for all four game modes, including lootboxes that can net you Goldbars, 1 day Lucky's and even a rare item that can't be found elsewhere - plus Experience & Gigas rewards.
